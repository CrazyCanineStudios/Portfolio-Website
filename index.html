What is Sleepless Nights?

Sleepless Nights is a local top down 2D survival shooter game built for the web using Phaser 3.
The goal of the game is to defeat the monsters infesting the house and reach the end of the level. The game can be played alone or with a friend using local multiplayer.

For centuries, monsters have terrorised young children by lurking in the shadows of their bedroom, hiding under their beds and inside their closets, delivering many Sleepless Nights.
Tonight is the night that everything changes. One brave group of children will take a stand against the monsters and show them that they are not kidding around.
They will fight, not just for themselves but for every kid too scared to fight back.
