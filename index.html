Hello, I'm Kenneth Yorke.

I’m a Gameplay Programmer.

Equipped with four years of academic experience and over three and a half years of hands-on professional experience using Unity Engine. I’m currently working on Merge Rivals! as a Game Programmer at Electric Manta. I graduated with a First Class Honours Degree in Computer Games Development from the University of the West of Scotland in June 2020 and was awarded two Court Medals for (Best Overall Student Performance) for Academic Excellence.

On my journey, I’ve worked on many game projects including a variety of hyper-casual and casual mobile games, a Norse Meets Sci-Fi Hack and Slash Roguelite, and a 3D Platformer game about a sausage-obsessed dog.

Please have a look at my projects below and don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any questions.

Merge Rivals!

A Free-To-Play Casual Game (Team)

Merge Rivals! is a live-service PVP casual Mobile game where the player must merge Cannons and Tents to become stronger and spawn better Units. I joined the project as a Junior Programmer supporting the project by setting up new units,implementing gameplay functionality and creating editor tools such as the Unit Testing Room to allow designers to quickly test new Units in various scenarios. I was soon promoted to Mid-level and led development of the project and all functionality.
